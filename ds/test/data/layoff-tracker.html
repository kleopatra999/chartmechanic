Feb. 6: Weyerhaeuser (nyse: WY - news - people ) shuts down veneer and lumber mills in Alabama, affecting 300 workers.

Feb. 5: Estée Lauder (nyse: EL - news - people ) fires 6% of workforce (2,000 employees) as part of a four-year restructuring plan.

Feb. 5: Fortune Brands (nyse: FO - news - people ) subsidiary MasterBrand Cabinets lays off a third of its workers at Oregon plant.

Feb. 5: The Wall Street Journal--owned by News Corp. (nyse: NWS - news - people )--cuts 25 positions from newsroom.

Feb. 5: Botox maker Allergan (nyse: AGN - news - people ) offers first-ever consumer rebate on its wrinkle killer, posts quarterly profit decline and cuts 5% of staff (460 workers).
